Air Fryer Chicken

Description

This is an expertly crafted Air Fryer Chicken Meal created by yours truly in university because I eat the same thing 3 days a week

This meal contains 4 simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Sainsbury's Chicken
  • Rice
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • Sainsbury's Frozen Broccoli

Steps

  1. Chuck ya chicken on a cutting board and rub the seasoning all over that badboy
  2. Throw it in the air fryer for 20-25 mins depending on how ur feeling and how u like the chicken
  3. cook your rice however you like it
  4. Boil your frozen broccoli for the amount of time it says on the package
  5. throw it all together in a bowl and salt and pepper it then enjoy!
